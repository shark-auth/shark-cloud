| Plan | Price | MAI | Delegation Depth | Vault Connections | Support |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Self-hosted OSS | Free forever | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited | Community |
| Cloud Free | $0/mo | Up to 20,000 | Max depth 2 | 3 | Community |
| Cloud Pro | $49/mo | Up to 50,000 | Max depth 4 | 10 | Priority email |

## Notes
- **MAI** = Monthly Active Identities (humans + agents that authenticate in the billing window)
- Self-hosted is MIT-licensed, zero telemetry, no vendor lock-in
- Cloud tiers are managed infrastructure on Shark Cloud (coming 2026)
