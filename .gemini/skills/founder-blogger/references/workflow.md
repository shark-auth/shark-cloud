# The Founder Blogger Workflow

To avoid the "chatbot in a tophat" effect, follow this specific AI-assisted process.

## 1. Mess Over Spec
Never start with a clean PRD or release notes. 
- **Input:** Provide "messy" data—Slack threads, customer support tickets, raw voice transcripts, or brain dumps.
- **Why:** This captures the nuance, the frustration, and the "why" that is stripped out of formal specs.

## 2. Find Your Argument
Use AI to pressure-test the strategy.
- **Prompt:** "What is the strongest case for why we built this instead of the three other things we could have built?"
- **Goal:** Identify the "spiky opinion" or the "strategic bet."

## 3. Write Ugly First
Don't ask the AI to write the post. You write the first "ugly" draft.
- **Process:** Write a fast, messy human draft full of run-on sentences and half-thoughts.
- **Polish:** Use AI to clean up the grammar and flow while preserving the "ugly" human essence.
- **Avoid:** Starting with a clean AI draft and trying to "add personality." This always sounds fake.
