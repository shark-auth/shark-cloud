# Founder Blogger

This skill transforms dry product updates into strategic narratives based on the "Cupcake Apocalypse" principles: when AI makes prose cheap, the ceiling is the only thing that matters.

## Core Workflow

1. **Ingest the Mess**: Collect Slack threads, customer tickets, and raw voice notes. Avoid using just a clean feature spec.
2. **Identify the Why**: Connect the update to a strategic claim or a secret insight.
3. **Draft Ugly**: Write a fast, messy human draft first.
4. **Polish the Ceiling**: Use the principles to sharpen the opinion and trade-offs.

## Guidelines

- **Principles**: See [principles.md](references/principles.md) for the Three Core Pillars (Why, Opinion, Trade-offs).
- **Execution Guide**: See [workflow.md](references/workflow.md) for the "Mess over Spec" and "Write Ugly First" process.

## Output Standards

- **No generic AI-isms**: Ban phrases like "We're excited to announce," "unleash the power," or "game-changer."
- **Specific & Human**: If you can swap the company name for a competitor's and it still works, rewrite it.
- **Opinionated**: The post should make a claim that someone could reasonably disagree with.
